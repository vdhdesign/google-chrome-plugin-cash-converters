# Privacy Policy

**Cash Converters Buy Now Price** ("the Extension")

**Last updated:** June 21, 2025

## Overview

Cash Converters Buy Now Price is a browser extension that displays buy-now prices on listing pages at [shop.cashconverters.co.nz](https://shop.cashconverters.co.nz). This privacy policy explains what information the Extension handles and how that information is used.

The Extension is not affiliated with or endorsed by Cash Converters.

## Information the Extension Handles

### Data stored on your device

The Extension stores the following data locally in your browser using Chrome's extension storage. This data stays on your device and is not sent to the developer or any third party:

- **User preference:** whether the "Items with buy now only" filter is enabled.
- **Cached buy-now prices:** listing IDs and their buy-now prices, along with a timestamp, so the Extension does not need to re-fetch the same listing repeatedly. Cached prices expire after ten minutes.

### Data fetched from Cash Converters

When you browse listing pages on shop.cashconverters.co.nz, the Extension may fetch public listing pages from that same website to read buy-now price information shown on those pages. The Extension only contacts shop.cashconverters.co.nz and only for listings visible on pages you visit.

### Data the Extension does not collect

The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit:

- Your name, email address, phone number, or other contact details
- Account credentials or login information
- Payment or financial account information
- Precise location data
- Browsing history outside shop.cashconverters.co.nz
- Analytics or usage tracking data

The Extension does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## How information is used

Information handled by the Extension is used only to:

- Display buy-now prices on Cash Converters listing pages
- Apply your filter preference to hide or grey out listings without a buy-now price
- Cache recently fetched buy-now prices to improve performance

The Extension does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

## Data retention and deletion

- Cached buy-now prices are kept for up to ten minutes, after which they may be refreshed.
- Your filter preference is kept until you change it or remove the Extension.

You can delete all Extension data by:

1. Removing the Extension from Chrome (`chrome://extensions`), or
2. Clearing the Extension's storage in Chrome extension settings.

## Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions:

- **Storage:** to save your filter preference and cached buy-now prices locally on your device.
- **Access to shop.cashconverters.co.nz:** to read listing pages on that site and display buy-now price information.

## Children's privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13, and it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, open an issue in the Extension's GitHub repository or contact the developer through the support channel listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.
